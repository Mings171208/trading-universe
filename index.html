Chapter 03

Trading Strategies

Every trader has a unique edge. Explore the most powerful trading methodologies used by professionals worldwide.

⚡

Scalping

The fastest style of trading. Scalpers execute dozens to hundreds of trades per day, targeting tiny price movements of 1–10 pips. Requires lightning-fast execution, discipline, and deep market knowledge.

Seconds–MinutesAdvancedHigh Risk
☀️

Day Trading

Open and close all positions within a single trading day. No overnight risk. Day traders capitalize on intraday price swings using technical analysis, level 2 data and momentum signals.

Minutes–HoursIntermediateMed-High
🌊

Swing Trading

Hold positions for days to weeks to capture the "swing" of a trend. Ideal for traders who can't monitor screens all day. Combines technical and fundamental analysis for high-probability setups.

Days–WeeksBeginner-FriendlyMed Risk
🏔️

Position Trading

The long game. Hold trades for weeks, months or even years based on macroeconomic trends. The most passive strategy — relies heavily on fundamentals and big-picture market cycles.

Weeks–MonthsBeginnerLow-Med Risk
🏛️

ICT / Smart Money

Institutional Concepts (ICT) — trade alongside banks and hedge funds. Focuses on Order Blocks, Fair Value Gaps, Liquidity Sweeps, and market structure to find high-probability institutional entries.

Any TimeframeAdvancedVariable
🤖

Algorithmic Trading

Let code trade for you. Algorithms execute strategies based on predefined rules — backtested, optimized, and emotionless. Used by hedge funds and increasingly by retail traders via Python & EAs.

Any TimeframeExpertVaries
📰

News Trading

Trade the volatility around high-impact economic events — NFP, FOMC, CPI. Price moves violently on unexpected data. Requires fast reaction, tight risk management, and an economic calendar.

MinutesIntermediateVery High
↩️

Mean Reversion

Bet that extreme price moves will snap back to average. Uses Bollinger Bands, RSI oversold/overbought zones, and statistical deviations to find "rubber band" setups for counter-trend entries.

Hours–DaysIntermediateMed Risk
🔥

Momentum Trading

Ride the wave. When an asset is moving strongly in one direction, momentum traders jump in to ride that trend until it shows signs of exhaustion. "The trend is your friend" — until it ends.

Minutes–DaysIntermediateMed-High
Chapter 04

Risk Management

Profitable traders aren't just right more often — they control their losses better. Risk management is the #1 skill that separates professionals from amateurs.

1-2%

Max Risk Per Trade

Never risk more than 1-2% of your total account on a single position. This protects you from catastrophic losses.

1:3

Risk-to-Reward Ratio

For every $1 you risk, aim to make at least $3. This way, even winning only 40% of trades keeps you profitable.

SL

Always Use Stop Loss

A stop loss is non-negotiable. It automatically exits your trade if price moves against you beyond your defined risk level.

3-5

Max Daily Losses

Set a daily max drawdown limit. If you hit 3-5% loss in a day, stop trading. Revenge trading destroys accounts.

📔

Trade Journal

Log every trade: entry, exit, reasoning, emotions. Reviewing your journal reveals patterns and helps eliminate mistakes.

🧠

Psychology

Fear, greed, and FOMO are your biggest enemies. Build a process-based mindset focused on execution, not outcomes.

Chapter 05

Your Trading Roadmap

The journey from beginner to consistently profitable trader. Follow these steps and trust the process.

📚 Learn the Fundamentals

Study how markets work, what moves price, how to read candlestick charts, support & resistance, and basic order types. Build your foundation before risking real money.

01
02

🎯 Choose Your Strategy

Pick one strategy that matches your personality and schedule. Scalping requires full attention. Swing trading fits around a day job. Master ONE method before diversifying.

📊 Backtest & Paper Trade

Test your strategy on historical data (backtesting) and then practice in a demo account. Aim for at least 100 simulated trades before using real capital.

03
04

💰 Start Small & Real

Fund a small live account. The psychology of real money is completely different from demo. Start with amounts you can afford to lose while you build consistency.

📈 Scale Up Consistently

Once you achieve 3-6 months of consistent profitability, gradually increase your position sizes and account capital. Patience compounds into exponential growth.
